## üß† Insights

* **Arabica is the dominant coffee type**, consistently contributing the highest portion of total sales and profit.
* Sales reached their **peak in 2021**, indicating strong market growth before slightly normalizing in 2022.
* The **United States contributes the largest portion** of sales among the countries.
* Cities like **New York, London, Seattle, Portland, and Birmingham** show exceptional performance, appearing across top categories.
* Monthly data reveals **seasonal purchase behavior**, with spikes in early-year and holiday-season months.
* **Robusta and Liberica**, despite smaller market shares, show stable niche demand.
* Customers tend to prefer **Light and Medium roasts**, supporting trend insights from coffee retail markets.
* Significant variability exists between cities, showing strong opportunities for **targeted local marketing campaigns**.

## ‚úÖ Recommendations

* **Increase inventory and marketing for Arabica** during high-demand seasons to maximize revenue.
